Campaign Description

Much has been modified in this campaign. Blood Elves have new building models, new units
and most importantly: my own hero, Blood Grand Mage Ravael. He is a soul Fire Mage, his spells are not ordinary spells of Warcraft. He and his spells are in each chapter. Kael, too, has some changes with spells like Summon Fire minion.

Ravael- Grand blood Mage


Fire Slam-Slams the ground, dealing damage to enemy units

Fire Fury-Summons and launches 2 fireballs in an arc towards the targeted area. Fireballs which hit an ennemy will deal them 75 damages. If they collide, the explosion produced will double the damages in an AoE.

Engulfing Chains-Magically binds a target enemy unit, so that it cannot move or attack and takes 10 damage per second. Lasts 15 seconds.


Dark Soul- Spawns a powerful voidlord who can't move or attack and has an ability Soulshatter that spawns lesser voidwalkers from dead corpses. Lasts 90 seconds.






Code
