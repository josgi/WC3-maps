Sentinels Campaign: Terror of the Tides
Based on the work of The-Spoon and Blizzard Entertainment.

Contains the original maps of the Sentinel campaign: Terror of the Tides updated by me to allow two players to replay the epic journey of the overzealous Maiev Shadowswong and her mute side-kick, Saithis as they attempt to capture the enigmatic Illidan Stormrage.

You can still download the maps individually and replay any of them:

- Chapter One: Rise of the Naga
- Chapter Two: The Broken Isles
- Chapter Three: The Tomb of Sargeras
- Chapter Four: Wrath of the Betrayer
- Chapter Five: Balancing the Scales
- Chapter Six: Shards of the Alliance
- Chapter Seven: The Ruins of Dalaran
- Chapter Eight: The Brothers Stormage

Features a number of systems: shared powerups, bounty, techtree, upgrades, infromation, tribute commands, tweaked upkeep levels and food limit, slow harvesting. (See Useful Links for more information.)

Important Information: The 2 Player Campaign is a concept first brought to Warcraft III by The-Spoon. The idea is to convert the original maps of Blizzard Entertainment so that it can be played by two people. The-Spoon, however, only wishes to complete the Reign of Chaos campaigns. Therefore, I have decided to do the Sentinel campaign.
